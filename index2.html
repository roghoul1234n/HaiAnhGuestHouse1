About

Hai Anh GuestHouse is located in Ham Ninh Fishing Village. We have about 16 rooms. Our room are clean and convenient. In each room, we will provide for you a television (TV), a fridge, a bathroom, a fan, an air conditioner and a wardrobe. This guesthouse is only 6 km drive from Phu Quoc International Airport. Tranh Waterfall is about 4 km away while Ham Ninh Fishing Village is only 300 metres away.
